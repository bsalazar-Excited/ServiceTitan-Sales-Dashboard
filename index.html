Frequently asked
💬 ARPU is below $285 — what do I do?›
Three levers: (1) Push Works over Essentials before pricing reveal. (2) Close at 2-year term — ramp pricing protects ARPU at month 18. (3) Quantify ROI before pricing — deals where dollar ROI is stated before pricing average $293/tech. Without it: $271. Discount via ramp or free months, not per-tech cuts.
💬 How do I compete against BuildOps?›
BuildOps is construction-focused. ST wins: (1) Service + construction hybrid is stronger for shops doing both. (2) Titan Intelligence AI ahead of BO. (3) OB and support reputation — BO churn is a real signal. Frame it: "BO is great if you're 100% construction. If you're running any service side, we're built for that entire lifecycle."
💬 How do I handle a prospect also looking at FieldEdge?›
600+ shops have switched from FE to ST. FE is residential-first. ST wins: commercial job costing depth, Works inventory, construction module, mobile offline, no office staff ceiling. Frame before they go in: "Here's what to compare when you talk to them next week."
💬 When should I DQ or move to off-books?›
DQ: fewer than 4 managed techs, no full-time office staff, no computer-based dispatch willing to adopt, 3+ construction projects over 3 months AND 4+ subs. Off-books: >30% commercial, >30% construction, >30% PM. Flag in SFDC notes with the specific reason so the ACP/ICP disposition is clean. Juan’s ask: always note the disposition reason in your SFDC notes template.
💬 What should a pre-call email look like?›
Per Juan: address by name, set agenda in 3 parts. (1) First 5-10 min: quick intros, learn their background. (2) Next 10-15 min: walk me through how the business runs today. (3) Remaining time: show what ST looks like for your specific setup. No slides language. End with "just a real conversation." Keep it under 150 words. This is about setting the table, not selling.
💬 Does ServiceTitan integrate with Sage?›
Sage Intacct: integration exists through partner-enabled accounting integrations. Sage 50: no native integration. If the prospect is on Sage, flag it in SFDC and route through #ask-partner-enabled-accounting-integrations. Don't present as a blocker — most commercial shops move their accounting to QBO as part of the ST onboarding.
💬 How does the QuickBooks data migration work?›
Initial pull from QBO at onboarding start. Rep keeps using QB until ST is fully built, then final transfer at go-live. QBO stays as accounting/GL. After final pull, any new data entered in QB after that date has to be manually entered in ST. Set clear expectations about the final pull date.
💬 When should I send the referral email?›
Week 3 of onboarding — customer is engaged and seeing value but not yet fully independent. Short, personal, ask for one warm intro. Never use a template that sounds like a template.
