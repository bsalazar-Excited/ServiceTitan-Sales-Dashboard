Frequently asked

    
💬 ARPU below $285 — what do I do?›
Three levers: (1) Push Works over Essentials — every Essentials deal costs $5-20/tech/month in lost ARPU. Make the Works ask before pricing reveal. (2) Close at 2-year terms — ramp pricing protects ARPU. (3) Quantify ROI before pricing — deals where dollar ROI is stated before pricing average $93/tech. Without it: $71. Discount with ramp or free months, never per-tech cuts.

    
💬 How do I compete against BuildOps?›
BuildOps over-indexes on construction, under-delivers on service. ST wins when any service work is in the mix. BO churn is real. Frame: "BO is great if you are 100% construction. Any service side and we are built for that entire lifecycle."

    
💬 Construction re-route threshold?›
Re-route if construction revenue is over 30% of total. If 31-99%: ask how many of every 5 projects last 3+ months AND how many subcontractors per project. If 3+ projects over 3 months AND 4+ subs — DQ.

    
💬 Minimum tech count to sell?›
Minimum 4 sellable licenses with at least 1 Managed Tech. All full-time (35+ hrs/week). In Commercial Construction: at least 1 crossover tech working 20%+ service jobs. DQ if fewer than 4.

    
💬 FieldEdge — how do I win?›
600+ shops switched from FE to ST. FE is residential-first — hits a ceiling on commercial. ST wins on: commercial job costing depth, Works inventory, mobile offline, no office staff ceiling. Frame before they talk to FE: "Here is what to ask when you talk to them."
